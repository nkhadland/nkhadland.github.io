                            I am a planetary scientist interested in <strong>microbial ecology</strong> and leveraging those insights to inform astrobiological investigations. My expertise spans environmental microbiology and geobiology across various settings, with a particular focus on volcanic systems as analogs for early Earth and Mars.

                        </p>
